GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) compliance

  • To ensure GDPR compliance without collecting or using any private data, we have implemented the following policies:
  • Collection of non-personal data: We only collect non-personal data that is necessary for the operation of our business. This includes information about the device and browser used to access our website, the pages viewed, the time spent on our site, and other similar metrics.
  • Consent: We do not require any consent from our website visitors as we do not collect any personal data.
  • Data storage: We do not store any data on our servers except for the non-personal data collected during a website visit. This data is stored in an encrypted format and is only accessible to authorized personnel.
  • Data sharing: We do not share any data with third-party companies or individuals.
  • Cookies: We use cookies on our website to improve the user experience. However, we do not use any cookies that track personal data.
  • Data access and deletion: As we do not collect any personal data, we do not have any data to provide access to or delete.
  • Security: We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of the non-personal data we collect.
  • We hope this policy provides clarity on how we ensure GDPR compliance while not collecting or using any personal data. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.


Cyborg AI for LinkedIn:



https://www.linkedin.com/in/cyborg-ai-308086271/

Step 1: Invite Cyborg AI to your LinkedIn account

Log in to your LinkedIn account and navigate to the "My Network" tab.
Click on "Connections" and then select "Add a connection".
In the search bar, type in "Cyborg AI" and select the Cyborg AI account.
Click on the "Connect" button and send a personalized message if desired.

Step 2: Write in chat with Cyborg AI

Once you have connected with Cyborg AI, you can begin writing in the chat.
Keep in mind that Cyborg AI is an AI-powered chatbot and cannot provide personalized advice or recommendations.
To get started, type your message in the chat and Cyborg AI will respond with suggestions or guidance based on your query.

Disclaimer

While using the chat feature, it is important to remember not to share any personal information in the chat.
Cyborg AI is designed to keep your data secure and confidential, but it is still important to exercise caution when sharing information online.
To ensure that your data is protected, we recommend that you delete the chat history from your side once your conversation with Cyborg AI is complete.
Remember that Cyborg AI is here to help you optimize your LinkedIn presence and achieve your goals. If you have any questions or concerns about using the platform, feel free to contact our support team for assistance.
